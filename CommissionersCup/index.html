The Game: 2-Man Scramble

  • Both players tee off, pick the best shot, and both play from there until holed out.
  • Each team gets 1 Breakfast ball on the first tee and 1 additional mulligan to use during the round.
  • If a team has a handicap, it will be subtracted from their final gross score.
  • In the event of a tie, a beer/cider sculling competition will be held at the 19th hole. Each team nominates one player to participate; the fastest scull wins.
Competition Prize: The winning team shall be appointed Commissioner and Treasurer respectively. They also reserve the right to nominate successors to take their place.
